SCHOOL TRANSPORT

COMSARE Logistics & Construction Limited > Services > School Transport

FLEXIBLE SOLUTIONS AT FIXED PRICES

COMSARE transport is a transportation service exclusively created to transport children to and from school, day care, after school activities, recreational programs etc.

Unlike a taxi service, COMSARE transport will provide transportation exclusively to children in a safe and responsible manner. All our drivers will have a background check through the Ghana police service for any infraction of the law. These drivers will possess a driver’s license. They also must genuinely care about the welfare of the children.

SCHOOL BUS POLICY AND RULES

COMSARE TRANSPORT DEPARTMENT provides school bus transportation. Drivers will make every effort to arrive promptly for school arrival and dismissal. He is conscious of the need to pickup and drop off pupils in a timely fashion.

It is therefore the responsibility of the parents/guardians to review these rules with your child/children. He /she is expected to always know and follow them.

Procedure

  • Place your child / children on the bus in the morning and meet him /her at the drop off point in the evening.
  • A parent/guardian/adult must stay with the child until he/she boards the bus in the morning.
  • Be on time for the afternoon pickup.
  • Instruct your child as to his or her pickup and drop off point.
