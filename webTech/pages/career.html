HELPING YOU REACH YOUR FULLEST POTENTIAL

COMSARE Logistics and Construction Limited is one of Ghana’s leading transport companies with core products such as logistics, freight-forwarding, goods transport and hiring of construction equipment in Ghana.

We are a medium-sized, ever-moving and dynamically growing Ghana transport company. Through active, close dialogue with our customers, we continuously develop new models for the secure storage, handling and transport of goods.

COMSARE Logistics and Construction Limited ambition is to be quality-conscious and oriented towards the future. This requires us – among other things – to maintain a high level of service, provide individualized solutions in terms of both products, and administration and to continuously develop our expertise.

Without skilled, dedicated employees we could never provide such quality. Without skilled, dedicated employees we could never ensure the continuous development of our business. This means that our employees are our most important asset. Accordingly, the vision of AGAS Logistics and Construction Limited is to be an attractive workplace.

COMSARE Logistics and Construction Limited is frequently on the lookout for new employees and gladly accepts unsolicited applications. Although you cannot be certain that we have a job opening when you apply, we will gladly keep your application on file. Therefore, if you are interested in working in a leading, dynamic transport company, we have lots to offer.

    COMSARE Logistics and Construction Limited provides:

  • An attractive, stimulating working environment, both professionally and socially.
  • New challenges every day in a dynamically growing company.
  • Good prospects of personal and professional development.
  • Motivating, loyal and optimistic colleagues.
  • Roughly dispersed over three locations in Ghana: Tarkwa, Takoradi and our head office, situated in Accra.
